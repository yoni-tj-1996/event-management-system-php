Privacy Policy for Event Management System


Proposal Effective Date: [Insert Date] This Privacy Policy outlines how the Event Management System (referred to as "the System") proposed by the Department of Software Engineering at Haramaya University collects, uses, and protects the information of its users. This policy applies to all users of the System, including event organizers, participants, and other stakeholders.

1. Information We Collect The System may collect the following types of information: Personal Information: This includes names, email addresses, phone numbers, and other contact details provided during the registration process. Event Information: Details about events, such as event names, dates, locations, and schedules. Payment Information: If applicable, payment details for event registration fees, which will be processed securely through a third-party payment gateway. Usage Data: Information on how users interact with the System, such as login times, pages visited, and features used. Feedback and Surveys: Responses provided by users in post-event surveys or feedback forms.

2. How We Use Information The information collected by the System will be used for the following purposes: Event Management: To facilitate event registration, scheduling, and communication between organizers and participants. Communication: To send automated reminders, updates, and notifications related to events. Improvement of Services: To analyze user feedback and usage data to improve the System's functionality and user experience. Analytics: To generate reports and insights on event attendance, participant engagement, and other metrics to help organizers make data-driven decisions.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure The System will not share or disclose personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances: With User Consent: Information may be shared with third parties if the user explicitly consents to such sharing. Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, hosting services) to assist in operating the System. These providers will have access to information only to the extent necessary to perform their functions and are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of the information. Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security The System is committed to protecting the security of user information. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include: Encryption: Sensitive data, such as payment information, will be encrypted during transmission. Access Controls: Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only. Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

5. User Rights Users of the System have the following rights regarding their personal information: Access: Users can request access to the personal information we hold about them. Correction: Users can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information. Deletion: Users can request the deletion of their personal information, subject to certain legal and contractual obligations. Opt-Out: Users can opt-out of receiving non-essential communications, such as promotional emails.

6. Data Retention We will retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. After the retention period, information will be securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting their information.
